Questions

Short Faqs

  • Which levels of damage can you repair?

    We specialize in repairing minor, moderate, and major damage to vehicles, ensuring comprehensive restoration. From cosmetic issues to structural repairs, our skilled technicians are equipped to handle it all. Trust us to get your vehicle back on the road in top condition.

  • How long will the repair process take?

    The duration of the repair process depends on the extent of the damage and the specific services required. Our team works diligently to complete repairs efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards. Upon assessment, we will provide you with an estimated timeline for completion.

  • What should I do immediately after a collision?

    After a collision, prioritize safety by checking for injuries and moving to a safe location if possible. Exchange insurance and contact information with the other party involved. Document the scene by taking photos and gathering witness information. Finally, contact emergency services and notify your insurance provider to begin the claims process.

  • Can I choose my collision repair shop?

    Yes, you have the right to choose your preferred collision repair shop. It's important to select a reputable and certified repair facility that meets your needs and provides quality service. Your insurance company may offer recommendations, but ultimately, the choice is yours.
